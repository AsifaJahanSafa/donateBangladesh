Donate for Flood at Feni, Bangladesh

Southeastern Bangladesh is currently enduring a devastating flood crisis due to continuous heavy rainfall and the rising levels of rivers. Regions of Feni, Comilla, and Noakhali are among the hardest hit, where entire communities have been cut off as roads and highways are submerged, leaving over 1.5 million people stranded.JAAGO Foundation is stepping in to provide critical relief in these flood-stricken areas. Through our youth wing, Volunteer for Bangladesh, we are distributing dry foods and providing medical assistance in Feni, Comilla, and Noakhali regions. Once the waters recede, our volunteers will work tirelessly to rehabilitate the affected families.
